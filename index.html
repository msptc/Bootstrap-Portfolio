About Me

My name is Masen Shreve and I attend Georgia Institute of Technology’s Full Stack Web Development professional certificate program. I graduated from Oglethorpe University with a bachelor’s degree in 2011. I graduated from Mercer University’s Walter F. George School of Law with a Juris Doctor degree in 2015.

While attending law school, I worked in several high-volume law firms that required great attention to detail under very strict time constraints. After graduating from law school, I decided that I would like to find a career that was more organic and allowed me to be more creative than practicing law. I enjoy both front and back end coding because allows for more creativity and it is something about which I am passionate.
